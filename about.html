How Does FutureFundr Work?

September 7th, 2014 by Chris

FutureFundr works the same as te stock market. Only, we invest in individuals rather than corporations (they're supposed to be the same thing right?). Our goal is to provide support for the person who embodies a significant cause that people can get behind. Our goal is to disrupt the convential means of funding an education, small busness, or otherwise.

What we do is perform a qualitative assessment on an individual to evaulate certain characteristics intrinsic to their being. We want to accurately measure things like resourcefulness and determination which are better predicting factors for success than grades or a nice suit. This assessment is added to their profile along with qualitative factors and achievements such as an art portfolio, blog articles, a Github repository, or otherwise.

Success is walking from failure to failure with no loss of enthusiasm. ~Winston Churchill

From the profile page, an individual can choose to support them and support their cause.

Disrupt the System

A lot of the time, people can never acheive greatness because they are too busy achieving mediocrity.

Get out of the rat race

Our goal is to provide funding to exceptional causes that will further the progress of society by allowing them to live their cause and not devote their time to furthering the adgenda of another
